TITLE: Community Health Scheme Eligibility Guide

Scheme A - Free Chronic Care Program: Covers free monthly medication for diabetes,
hypertension, and asthma for residents with a household income below the local low-income
threshold. Required documents: locality residence proof and income certificate. Renewal is
required every 12 months at any listed community health centre.

Scheme B - Maternal & Child Wellness Support: Provides free prenatal checkups, iron and
folic acid supplements, and postnatal home visits by a community health worker for pregnant
residents. Registration is open at any maternal care clinic and should ideally happen in the
first trimester.

Scheme C - Senior Citizen Home Visit Program: Residents aged 65 and above with mobility
limitations can request a free home visit from a community health worker for basic checkups,
medication review, and vaccination reminders. Requests can be made via the citizen helpline
or the assistant.

Scheme D - Vaccination Camp Access: All residents can access free vaccination camps
regardless of income. Camp schedules are published weekly and can be requested by locality.
